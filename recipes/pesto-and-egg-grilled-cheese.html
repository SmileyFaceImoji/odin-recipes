Pesto and Egg Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Light and airy scrambled eggs are layered
with creamy provolone cheese and flavorful
pesto for a quick and substantial sandwich
that will redefine what it means to be grilled cheese.

Ingredients

  • 2 tsp Olive Oil
  • 2 large Beaten Eggs
  • 2 slices Whole Grain Bread
  • 1 tbsp Basil Pesto
  • 1 slice Provolone cheese

Steps

  1. In a medium non-stick skillet,
    heat olive oil over medium heat
  2. Pour eggs into hot skillet and gently
    pull the eggs across the pan using a spatula
  3. Cook until large soft curds occur,
    and no liquid remains. Set aside.

  5. In the same skillet over medium heat, place 1 slice, plain side down,

  6. inish with the other slice of bread.
  7. Cook 3-5 minutes on each side until golden and cheese is melted
